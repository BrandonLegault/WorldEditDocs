.. topic:: Butcher flags

    .. csv-table::
        :widths: 3, 15
        
        **Flag**,**Description**
        ``-p``,Kills tamed pets
        ``-n``,Kills NPCs
        ``-g``,Kills golems
        ``-a``,Kills animals
        ``-b``,Kills ambient mobs
        ``-t``,Kills mobs with name tags
        ``-f``,Combines all of the above flags
        ``-r``,Kills armor stands
